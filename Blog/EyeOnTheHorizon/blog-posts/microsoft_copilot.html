Microsoft's vision for Copilot is to provide users with a seamless AI experience that integrates into their daily workflow. Here are some of the significant features highlighted during the presentation:

    Copilot will be seamlessly integrated into Windows 11, making it readily accessible with a single click.   3. Ink Anywhere
    A groundbreaking feature revealed is the ability to use a pen to ink in any text box anywhere in Windows 11. Copilot enhances this capability by instantly solving complex math equations when written, making tasks like mathematical calculations a breeze.
  4. Personalized Shopping
    Copilot's utility extends to shopping, helping users make informed decisions. It can guide you through the shopping process by asking smart questions, comparing options, providing reviews, and even finding the best prices and online promo codes, all within Microsoft Edge.

    With the latest Dali model, Copilot offers an image description feature that allows users to describe the image they want and receive stunning options quickly. This makes content creation more accessible and efficient.
  6. Personalized Answers
    Copilot goes beyond just providing information; it personalizes results based on your chat history and previous interactions. This feature was showcased in the presentation when a user asked for weekend plans, and Copilot considered their preferences and interests to deliver tailored results.
  7. Copilot Lab
